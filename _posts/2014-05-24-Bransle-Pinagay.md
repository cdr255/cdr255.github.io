To perform this dance, You need a line or a circle of dancers.

    ====
    Section A:  1  - 4    Double left,
	            5         Kick left.
		        6  - 9    Double left,
		        10 - 12   Kick left, right, left.
    ====
    Section B:  13 - 16   Double left,
                17 - 20   Double right.
				21 - 24   Double left,
				25 - 28   Double right.
    ====
